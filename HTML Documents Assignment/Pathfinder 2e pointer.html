Pathfinder 2nd edition mention




Now I honestly don't know much about pathfinder 2nd edition, what I do know that it's a good bit simpler than the 1st! So if you're new to pathfinder, or roleplaying in general, it's an easier place to begin for both players and game masters. The only major drawback, it's designed for short game sessions and lacks some of the depth and built in customization from it's previous iteration.
