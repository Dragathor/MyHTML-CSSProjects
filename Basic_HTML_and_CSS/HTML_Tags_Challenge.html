Pathfinder a masters guide!

One thing I want you to keep in mind above all else. Your mind and imagination are unique, this is your superpower! USE IT!!! The wonderful thing about fantasy is you can change anything on a whim. Don't like somthing? POOF it's gone. Think that idea will be awsome? ADD IT IN, change the script, you are in control you are the GAME MASTER

Tips

Use your players!

Specifically use their inaginations to assist you in running your game. If you create a puzzle or problem (or use one from a pre writtem adventure) and you think "My players ideas are WAY cooler than the mundane solution I have here", just run with it! Some of the coolest experiences I've had running games have come from my players wacky and wierd solutions where I step back and let their own solutions change the narritive, see where the madness takes you!
