The Greenheart Protectorite

Places of interest in the Protectorite!

Where would you like to go worthy Adventurer?

Fort Tuskwater (CITY)

The Capital of the Protectorite. May you seek glory and fame in the kingdoms name!!!

Olegsberg (CITY)

The trade centre of the lands. Money and wealth is it you seek?

Tatzlford (TOWN)

Fortress Town and last refuge before the frontier. Specializing in herb cultivation with wonderful hot springs.

Varnhold (TOWN)

A peaceful farming and livestock Town. With a nomadic Centaur Village in the area.

Dwarfhold "Dag'goraz'thath" (CITY)

A dwarven city decicated to the god Droskar. May toil lead you to success!

Fort Drelev (TOWN)

Is it to be the dangerous untamed frontier Fortress Town? Plenty of adventure and glory left to claim!

Tempel Refuge of Erastil

A peaceful life of reflection and prayer for the weary soul. Stunning scenery in a secluded forest.

Scalehold (VILLAGE)

A swampy village of Kobolds and Lizardmen. Take care where you step as the waters go deep...

Nomen Tribe (VILLAGE)

Centaur Nomads of the great planes. Wary of outsiders but willing to trade meat and textiles for what they need.

Meet the Council of the Protectorite!

Harkon Pegason

The leader of the council and fearless paladin. Justice and unity above all is sought, unafraid to make the harshest of decisions to protect the people.

Catheshal Jaet

Lead diplomat and fey like enchantress. All fall to their knees in awe at her beauty and grace.

Oleg Leventen

The only normal member of the group. A regular frontier shopkeeper elevated to the treasurer of an entire empire. Constantly under the immense stress assisting in leading with mad adventurers.

Finbar Garress

Ruthless military warlord and commander of all forces in the Protectorate. Valuing loyalty and perfection and punishing failure with violence.

Harlos Fury

Head of faith and fiery of personality. Being a lava gnome she struggles with the passions and sensibilities of humans. Her fiery sermons often rouse religious fervour and aggression in ways she really doesn't mean or expect.

Varik Surtova

Spymaster and problem solver... Hilariously due to his very private and secretive nature he is the most beloved. Acting as a poor herbalist being dragged about with the adventurers on the council. He ensures sponsoring for hospitals, orphanages and healers throughout the kingdom (a good portion of which are also spies and assassins).
